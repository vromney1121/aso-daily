Google I/O resets the GEO board: Gemini 3.5 Flash powers AI Mode, the search box is rebuilt, and Universal Cart goes live

Four announcements in one keynote change the brief for every GEO program. Entity consistency just stopped being a hygiene concern and became the entire surface.
